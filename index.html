How can I add my own handwriting?

+

Follow our step-by-step guide above to create and upload your custom handwriting font. We recommend using Calligraphr for the best results.

What font formats are supported?

+

We support .ttf and .otf font formats. These are the most common formats for custom fonts.

How do I adjust text spacing?

+

Use the 'Word Spacing' and 'Letter Spacing' options in the customization panel to adjust the spacing between words and letters.

Can I save my generated text?

+

Yes! You can download your generated text as an image or save multiple pages as a PDF using the download buttons.
